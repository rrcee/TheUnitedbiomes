Protect your username the first time you join, then log in on future visits.

Account Registration

When connecting for the first time, open in-game chat and create your password.

/register password password
Type your chosen password twice. Avoid spaces.
02

Subsequent Logins

Every time you reconnect later, authenticate yourself to unlock movement.
